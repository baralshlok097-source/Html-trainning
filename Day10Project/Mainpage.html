Welcome to Budhanilkantha Secondary English School

Budhanilakantha Secondary English School is a well-known English-medium educational institution located in Shankarpur, Biratnagar-2, Morang, Nepal. Established in 2040 B.S. (1984 A.D.), the school has been dedicated to providing quality education for more than four decades. It offers education from Nursery (Play Group) to Grade 10 in a safe, disciplined, and student-friendly learning environment.

The school emphasizes both academic excellence and the overall development of students through extracurricular activities, sports, science exhibitions, robotics, and cultural programs. It provides modern facilities such as well-equipped science and computer laboratories, a library, multimedia classrooms, transportation, and hostel facilities to support effective learning.

With its experienced teachers, quality education, and commitment to character building, Budhanilkantha Secondary English School has earned a strong reputation as one of the respected schools in Biratnagar, preparing students to become responsible, confident, and successful citizens.
